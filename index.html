Tinghui Zhu

School of Computer Science, Fudan University

I am a second year Master student at School of Computer Science, Fudan University, a member of Knowledge Works Research Laboratory, supervised by Professor Yanghua Xiao. Currently, I am lucky to be a research intern at LUKA Group, working with Professor Muhao Chen. Previously, I was a research intern at OSU NLP Group, working with Professor Yu Su.

My research interests are broadly in Natural Language Processing and Multimodality, focusing on Reasoning, Planning, and RAG across different modalities.
